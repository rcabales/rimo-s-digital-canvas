## The Client

Hassan Nur is a 3D digital artist based in London, England. He has a strong passion for games and aims to apply his expertise in 3D art and quality assurance within the gaming industry. His personal website showcases his work.

## The Project & What Did I Do?

The client needed a website to showcase his latest and best work to potential employers and clients, with a priority on speed and a simple, media-focused structure capable of handling a large volume of images and video.

I served as the sole developer on the project, implementing [Sanity.io](https://sanity.io) as the CMS and building the front end with React.js. To optimize performance and efficiency, videos were hosted on YouTube and embedded throughout the site.
