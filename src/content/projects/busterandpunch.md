## The Client

Buster + Punch is a London-born home fashion label founded in 2012 by architect and industrial designer Massimo Buster Minale. The company specializes in transforming everyday functional fittings into extraordinary home details, working with rare, solid metals to create unique products.

The brand draws inspiration from London's fashion, music, and sub-culture scenes, collaborating with various artists and designers to infuse attitude into their crafted products.

As of 2025, Buster + Punch employs approximately 87 people and has received venture capital backing, with the latest investment amounting to $3.64 million.

## The Project & What Did I Do?

The client wanted to refresh their entire site and, after exploring several prototypes, decided on WordPress with WooCommerce for the backend.

My role focused on developing the non-e-commerce sections, primarily the blog, where I handled both front-end and back-end development. Working closely with designers and project managers, I also played a key role in shaping the site's design language, ensuring a cohesive and engaging user experience.
