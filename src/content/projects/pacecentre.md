## The Client

The Pace Centre, established in 1990, is a UK-based charity and specialist school located in Aylesbury, Buckinghamshire. It is dedicated to supporting children and young people aged 0-18 with neurodisabilities, such as cerebral palsy.

![Pace Centre screenshot](/projects/pacecentre/pacecentre1.png)

## The Project & What Did I Do?

The client required a complete redesign and rebuild of their site to elevate both their online presence and branding. I served as the lead on the project, overseeing both the development and project management.

![Pace Centre screenshot](/projects/pacecentre/pacecentre2.png)

I was supported by an additional developer who focused on a select number of complex features, enabling me to maintain momentum on the broader build while effectively managing the overall project.
