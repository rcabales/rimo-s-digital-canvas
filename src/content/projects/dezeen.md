## Dezeen

### The client
Dezeen is a prominent online magazine focusing on architecture, interiors, and design, headquartered in London. Established in November 2006 by Marcus Fairs, it has grown into a leading platform in the design industry.

### The project + what did I do?
The client needed a total redesign, opening the door for a complete rebuild of the site from the ground up and allowing us to shed a lot of obsolete legacy code that had been hanging around for years. I paired with a back end developer and the two of us built the new version of the site, with me working predominantly on the front end.
